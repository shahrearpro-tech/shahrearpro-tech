### `whoami`

I'm a full-stack developer and technical project lead at **Innovative IT Solutions** — a digital agency in Chittagong, Bangladesh.

I don't just write code. I sit with clients, understand how their **business actually runs**, then figure out what to build. That approach has led me to build everything from clean marketing sites to full-blown **ERPs for garments factories**.

Lately I've been going deep into the **RMG (Ready-Made Garments)** sector — Bangladesh's biggest industry, and one that's largely underserved by good software.

On the side, I'm finishing a **Masters in Business Finance**. Understanding the business side changes how you architect things.

> *"Kono system build korar age, amar client-er sob process notebook-e likhi. Tarpor code kori."*
